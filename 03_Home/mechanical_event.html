Quadra 2.20

Department : MECHANICAL

Team Members : The team should not consist of more than 4 members.

Fees per Student : ₹ 50 Per Person

Event Description :

  • The robots build by given dimension and specification will be allowed for game. The rounds will be decided according to groups participated.
  • The team should not consist of more than 4 members.
  • Each member from same college is not mandatory.
  • The robot should follow the robot specifications provided. Any deviation from the mentioned specifications will lead to disqualification.
  • Lego kits are strictly not allowed in any form. The L x B x H should not exceed 300 x300 x 300 mm. The weight of robot should not exceed 2 kg. (excluding remote and battery)
  • The team would consist of minimum 2 member. The structure of the robot should not be changed during the competition.
  • If the robot by any chances falls from the bridge, the robot will be kept back to the previous checkpoint crossed with a penalty.
  • During the game play, if any part of a robot is destructed/ dismantled/ damaged the participant will be given a timeout to repair at an expense of a penalty, while the next participant for will be called the play.

Volcanus 2.0

Department : MECHANICAL

Team Members : 2 – 4 participants

Fees per Student : ₹ 50 Per Person

Event Description :

  • The Contest judges may stop any robot at any time if they feel that it is performing, or is about to perform, any action that is dangerous or hazardous to people or equipment.
  • All Electronic Circuitry must be designed and fabricated by the Contestants themselves.
  • Combat robotics is dangerous. All participants build and operate robots at their own risk. Please take care to not hurt yourself or others when building, testing and competing.
  • For a particular match, both teams would face the same conditions. Winning would be dependent on no.., of fall from platform and also maximum damage to the opponent.
  • After the blow of whistle, the robot would have 3 minutes to complete with other robot or best out of 3 would also be considered.
  • During its operation robot is NOT allowed to extend any extension/support for any purpose, the area of the robot should remain the same throughout the operation i.e. it should fit in 500 x 300 x 250 mm.
  • The robot must fit within 500 x 300 x 250 mm at all times of it operation it is not allowed to extend any extensions at any time of its operation .All robots will be carefully measured. All weapons on the robot will be counted as part of the robot’s total dimensions.
  • All robots must have easily visible and controlled mobility in order to compete. Methods of mobility include:
  • Walking (linear actuated legs with no rolling or cam operated motion). Rolling (wheels, tracks or the whole robot). Ground effect air cushions (hovercrafts) is allowed. Shuffling (rotational cam operated legs)
