Aqua Craft

Department : CIVIL

Team Members : Maximum 4 person and minimum 2 person can participate.

Fees per Student : â‚¹ 50 Per Person

Event Description :

  • The AQUACRAFT is an event in which a participated group has to form a boat with the given material only i.e. ice cream stick, tape and glue. The formation of boat should be such that it could be able to float on the water. After that the weight will be added on it and the result will be declared on the basis of the maximum weight carried by the boat. The maximum weight a boat can handle the maximum chance of winning.
  • Time duration (45 min).
  • Given material should only be used.
  • On the spot disqualification in case any external material usage.
  • In case of tie between two groups i.e. a boat is carrying same weight and any further weight adding space is unavailable time duration will be counted. Maximum time a boat can carry a weight before sinking will have more chance of winning.
