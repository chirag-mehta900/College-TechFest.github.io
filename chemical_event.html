Paper Presentation

Department : CHEMICAL

Team Members : 2 participants

Fees per Student : ₹ 50 Per Person

Event Description :

  • This is a group event every student can participate in this event individually or in group.
  • Each group can consist of maximum 4 members. As the name of the event itself says, participants will be presenting a technical topic of their choice in front of the judges.
  • The participants have to make their presentations in IEEE format and have to give the presentation at the day of event in front of expert faculties who will judge the presentation.
  • The winner will be decided according to presentation that was being given taking into consideration the current facts, technical application, etc.

Aayam is the annual technical festival of BMU Surat. Aayam started in 2013. It has been witnessing active participation for 8 years and has seen the amalgamation of ideas and practical exposure.
